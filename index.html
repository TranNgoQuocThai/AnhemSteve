Hello my name is AnhemSteve

I am a Addon Creator for Minecraft Bedrock Edition.
I have been passionate about Minecraft since I first downloaded Minecraft on my mother's iPad 9 years ago. At that time, I felt this was truly the best sandbox game I had ever played.
During the process of playing Minecraft, I experimented a lot and discovered the game. I practiced being a Minecraft youtuber when I was in high school,
but after a while I ran out of ideas and I stopped being a Minecraft youtuber for a while. When I went to college, I started to learn about Minecraft files,
I copied some addons and edited them to my liking to play (just for myself).
When the covid 19 pandemic hit, I was stuck at home and I saved up money to buy Minecraft Window 10, which was an important turning point in my addon career.
After purchasing the copyright of Minecraft BE on Windows 10, I approached the Bridge v1 addon software. It helped me learn a lot in making addons later and that was the time I started making addons.
